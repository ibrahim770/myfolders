Somalia National AI Strategy

By Aidarus Mohamed

Background

The Somalia National AI Strategy is designed to guide the development and application of Artificial Intelligence technologies across the country. It promotes ethical innovation, capacity building, and inclusive digital growth.

Vision

To make Somalia a leader in ethical and inclusive AI deployment in East Africa by 2030.

Strategic Pillars

  • AI Talent Development & Education
  • Data Governance & Infrastructure
  • AI for Public Good (Healthcare, Agriculture, etc.)
  • AI Ethics & Regulations
  • Research, Innovation & Startups

Implementation Plan

A phased approach starting with awareness and training, followed by policy development, pilot programs, and scaling across sectors. Key partners include the Ministry of Communications, local universities, and international tech stakeholders.
