Somalia National AI Strategy

By Aidarus Mohamed

Background

The Somalia National AI Strategy is a milestone initiative supported by UN-ESCWA and the National Communications Authority of Somalia. Developed in response to the 2024 Somalia AI Summit, this strategy outlines a roadmap for national AI integration that enhances public service delivery, fosters innovation, and accelerates digital transformation across key sectors.

Vision

To position Somalia as a regional leader in ethical, inclusive, and impactful AI adoption by 2030.

Strategic Pillars

  • AI Talent Development & Education
  • Data Governance & Digital Infrastructure
  • AI for Public Good (Healthcare, Agriculture, Public Services)
  • Ethical AI Regulations & Responsible Use
  • AI Research, Innovation & Startup Ecosystem

Key Achievements

  • Drafted Somalia's first AI strategy based on global frameworks and local needs using SWOT and gap analysis tools.
  • Engaged 20+ national stakeholders through interviews and workshops, collecting critical insights into Somalia's AI landscape.
  • Led national workshop for multi-sectoral validation of the draft, ensuring stakeholder ownership and alignment.
  • Developed a 30+ page strategy document and 4-page executive summary, meeting ESCWA technical standards.
  • Incorporated gender equality and youth engagement across strategy objectives and language.

Impact

The strategy lays a foundation for Somalia to build a thriving AI ecosystem that drives job creation, digital innovation, and improved government efficiency. It connects local priorities with international AI governance best practices, making it a replicable model for other emerging economies.

Relevant Policy Contributions

  • Data Protection Policy – Somaliland National Insurance Agency
  • IT Policy – Amanah Insurance
  • System Usage Policy – Takaful Insurance
  • Somaliland Cybersecurity Act (Drafted)
  • Somaliland Data Protection Act (Drafted)
  • Business Process Re-engineering – Central Bank of Somaliland (Core Banking & National Payments)
