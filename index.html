About Me

I am a seasoned consultant specializing in Artificial Intelligence policy, digital transformation, and ICT governance. I bring over 5 years of hands-on experience supporting governments, regulatory bodies, and institutions across the Horn of Africa to build resilient, forward-looking digital strategies. I have contributed to high-level initiatives such as the Somalia National AI Strategy under the guidance of UN-ESCWA, where I led stakeholder engagement, gap analysis, and drafting the national AI framework.

My work bridges policy design and technical implementation. I've authored several policies and legislative frameworks, including the Somaliland Cybersecurity Act, Data Protection Policy, and national IT regulations. These efforts are geared towards enhancing data governance, promoting innovation, and ensuring inclusive access to emerging technologies.

In addition to national consulting, I have worked with financial institutions and insurance companies to improve digital operations, write IT and data protection policies, and lead business process re-engineering initiatives.
